## Sample 1 — "AI won't wait for you" (motivational, line-rhythm)
AI won't wait for you.
This is not a warning.
It is a reminder.
The biggest advantage today is not only having talent.
It is knowing how to combine your talent with AI.
A designer who uses AI can test more ideas.
A developer who uses AI can solve problems faster.
The real question is not: "Will AI replace me?"
The better question is: "Am I learning how to use AI before others do?"
Start small.
Use AI to brainstorm. Use AI to summarize. Use AI to improve your writing.
You do not need to become an AI expert overnight.
But you do need to start.
How are you using AI in your work or study today?
